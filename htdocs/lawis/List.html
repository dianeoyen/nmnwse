LAWIS LISTSERVER

Welcome to the Los Alamos Women in Science (LAWIS) discussion list. The purpose of this list is to facilitate communications among members of LAWIS.  Appropriate postings to the list include conference and meeting announcements, job postings, and other LAWIS-related business.  List subscribers who post inappropriate material will be unsubscribed by the list owner.

For those of you who have not been members of listserv lists at UNM before, what follows are instructions on how to post, unsubscribe, and get information about the list.We  hope this list will be a useful tool to members of LAWIS.

Instructions for the NMNWSE listserver can be found at:
        http://nmnwse.org/Listserver.html

TO SEND A MESSAGE TO THE LIST

Once members are subscribed, to communicate with one another simply address messages to
        lawis-l@list.unm.edu
You can also send messages to the list if you are not subscribed, but the message will have to be approved by the list manager.

 
TO SUBSCRIBE

Send a message To:
        listserv@list.unm.edu
Leave the Subject field blank.   In the body of message type (with no other text):
        subscribe lawis-l Firstname Lastname

 
TO UNSUBSCRIBE

Send a message To:
        listserv@list.unm.edu
Leave the Subject field blank.   In the body of message type (with no other text):
        signoff lawis-l

 
WHO OWNS THE LIST

Deborah Ulinski Potter, Past President NMNWSE, ulinski@unm.edu

If you have questions or experience problems, please contact the NMNWSE webmaster
        webmaster@nmnwse.org
or the current NMNWSE membership chair.

 
MORE INFORMATION ABOUT THE LIST

To get a document describing this listserver, Send a message to:
        listserv@list.unm.edu
Leave the Subject field blank.   In the body of message type (with no other text):
        info lawis-l

A detailed user's guide can be found at:
http://www.lsoft.com/manuals/user/user.html

Only list requests (subscribe, signoff, info, etc.) should be sent to listserv@list.unm.edu.
