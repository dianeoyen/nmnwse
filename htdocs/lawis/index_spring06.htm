MEMBERSHIP INFORMATION

The NMNWSE and LAWIS Membership Year is defined as from October 1 to September 30. LAWIS chapter dues are rebated from the state dues, so no separate dues are collected from LAWIS members. A copy of the membership form is available on the web at http://www.nmnwse.org in the "Membership in NMNWSE" section.
