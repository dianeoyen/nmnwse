1998 Los Alamos County
Science Fair Awards

LAWIS is proud to recognize the following participants in the 1998 Los Alamos County Science Fair, held on February 7, 1998, for their accomplishments in the study of science:

Top awards:
(gift certificate, "Trust Me, I'm a Scientist" bumper sticker, LAWIS certificate)

  • $25 - Katie Wurden , Pieces of Pi
  • $15 - Claire Lackner, Collecting CO2  from the Air
  • $10 - Kristin White, Fire Light, Fire Bright

Additional Awards:
($5 gift certificate, "Trust Me, I'm a Scientist" bumper sticker, LAWIS certificate)

  • Heather Altherr, Digging Magnetism
  • Jessica Chipera, Experimenting With Cars
  • Kiran Chitanvis, The Effect of Acid Rain on Plant Growth
  • Erin Dempsey, Comparison of Household Cleaners
  • Kristen Hemphill, Is It Food or Is It Water?
  • Betsy Nichols, Heart Throbs: A Comparison of Athletes, Guys and Gals
  • Micah Sims, Solar Hot Water Heater
  • Erin Smith, Water From Fire?!!!!
  • Jennifer Trent, Friction of Different Materials
  • Rima Turner (2 projects!), The Powers of the Towers of Hanoi
    and What Growing Medium Do Scarlet Runner Beans Like Best?
  • Kimberly Vela, Liquid Properties and Crystal Growth

We hope all participants had fun, and will continue to develop their potential as future scientists!
