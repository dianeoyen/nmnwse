The CAVE Immersive Visualization Facility is a 33 Panel, 5-wall stereo display. This facility is the jewel of the ASCII large scale visualization program and the most advanced facility of it's type in the world. The demonstration will feature visualizations of data sets from lab research that my team supports.  (LA-UR-05-6044)

Attendance is limited!
