Focus on Retirement
Marlene Vrba and Caroline Jansson, Morgan Stanley

This presentation is designed to assist professional women in rethinking, revisiting, refining and reviewing their retirement options based on the economic climate as well as their own personal factors - the 4 R's for a successful retirement. Both general considerations as well as changes one might want to consider in light of the new LANS plans will be addressed.

The room is reserved from 11:30 to 1:30 so people to can get together and network before and/or after the talk. Bring your lunch or pick up something at Hot Rocks Java Cafe on the 2nd floor. You are welcome to come early, late, or for just the talk at noon, and questions are encouraged!
Note: Snacks will be provided by Morgan Stanley!
