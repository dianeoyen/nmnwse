Background on the Class Action Lawsuit
Loyda G. Martinez, plaintiff

Two lawsuits against LANL on pay disparity issues for hispanics and women have been filed, and class action status requested. These were combined into one case and a decision on the class action request is pending. Loyda, a plaintiff in one of lawsuits speaking as a private citizen, will discuss some of the background on why the lawsuits were filed and current status. Allegations raised in the lawsuits include:

  • problems with the OCR score system for determing pay raises, and relation to the LLNL lawsuit finding inherent bias in a subjective performance scoring methodology
  • inadequacies of the Welch Report and LANL response to it
  • difficultly in obtaining information from LANL
