 Talk at 12:00, Tuesday, August 15, 2006

The LANL Class Action Lawsuit Settlement - Your Choices
Patrick D. Allen,   Yenson Lynn Allen & Wosick P.C.
John Bienvenu,   Rothstein Law Firm
Richard Hughes,   Rothstein Law Firm

This presentation is designed to assist females and Hispanics affected by the recent class action lawsuit understand their options defined in the settlement agreement.   After the lawyer's introductory remarks, the rest of the time will be spend on Q/A. See  http://www.lanlclassactionsettlement.com/  for more information on this class action settlement.
