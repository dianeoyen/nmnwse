ABSTRACT

"Career Options: A Student Focus"

LAWIS and the Women's Diversity Working Group (WDWG) are jointly sponsoring a panel discussion by women at Los Alamos National Laboratory. The panelists reflect both science and support positions, some with novel combinations of background. Participants will share, discuss, and answer questions about their educational and individual career paths. Everyone is welcome, especially our visiting summer students. Bring your questions and find out how these women achieved their chosen vocations!

Student Poster Session

There will be a Student Poster Session following shortly after the panel discussion. Whether or not you have time to attend the panel discussion, we encourage you to take time to browse through the posters sometime in the afternoon and see what the students have been doing! The panel and poster session take place in conjunction with a Job Fair sponsored by the LANL Student Programs Office and the LANL Student Association.
