Vera Vigil, ALDTR/NISDO, LANL
Trouble Shooting Computer Audio Problems
CANCELLED
ABSTRACT

This presentation will expose the audience to computer technical terms, peripherals, software and tools in regard to troubleshooting computer audio problems. It will also provide some hands on experience.
(Note this talk is an overview of an Expanding Your Horizons Workshop.)

Members and Nonmembers Welcome!
Open to the Public

The room is reserved from 11:30 to 1:30 so people to can get together and network before and/or after the talk. Bring your lunch or pick up something at the Hot Rocks Java Cafe on the 2nd floor. You are welcome to come early, late, or for just the talk at noon, and questions are encouraged!
