LUNCHTIME TALK SERIES
 
Tuesday, February 13, 2001
11:30 am - 1:30 pm, San Ildefonso Room, Study Center (upstairs), LANL
Complementary coffee and soft drinks will be served

TALK at 12:00:
Dolores Jacobs, Group Leader, HR-6-TD, LANL
Resources for Career Development

ABSTRACT

The presentation will focus on strategies for finding a mentor, tips for making the most of a mentoring relationship, and networking. Laboratory training resources for career development wil be discussed.

The originally scheduled speaker, Sue Bargeloh, was pre-empted by a reschedule of the Management Institute, which was cencelled by snow.

See also the LunchTalk by Carol Ann Martz, for more on Career Development.

Nonmembers Welcome!  

The San Ildefonso Room is reserved from 11:30 to 1:30 for informal discussion. Complementary coffee and soft drinks will be served. You are welcome to come early, late, or for just the talk at noon, and questions are encouraged!

For driving directions to the J. Robert Oppenheimer Study Center at TA-3, LANL, see: http://www.lanl.gov/tools/maps
(direct link to map showing the J. Robert Oppenheimer Study Center: http://nis-www.lanl.gov/cgi-bin/wthi?ta=3&bldg=207).
