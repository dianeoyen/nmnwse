LUNCHTIME TALK SERIES

Wednesday, May 5, 1999

LAWIS lunchtime Talk and get-together
11:30 am - 1:30 pm, Otowi cafeteria side room C, LANL

Talk at 12:00:
Morrison Bennett, LC-BPL, LANL
"Patentable Inventions"

ABSTRACT

Morrison will discuss how patentable inventions can be identified, what inventions are patentable, identification of inventors, what obligations inventors have to the Lab, and what patent attorneys do. If you have never had an invention patented but might someday, or you are simply curious about the process, this talk is for you!
