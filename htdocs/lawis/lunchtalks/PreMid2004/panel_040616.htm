Career Paths for Women in the Sciences and Science-Related Fields

LAWIS and the Student Programs Office are organizing this panel discussion as a mentoring opportunity for students. Panelists include:

  • Jessica Clark, Equipment Design Engineer, Merrick and Co
  • Morrison "Jib" Bennett, Patent Attorney, IM-1, LANL
  • Kimberly Thomas, Nuclear Chemist & Deputy C-Division Director, C-DO, LANL
  • Maya Gokhale, Computer and Information Scientist, ISR-3, LANL
  • Tinka Gammel, Theoretical Condensed Matter Physicist, T-1, LANL
If you are interested in being a panelist, contact Tinka Gammel, jtg@lanl.gov, 7-9149 or Carole Rutten, crutten@lanl.gov, 5-5194. If you are interested in attending, contact Liz Ramirez (eramirez@lanl.gov, 5-6345) to reserve your space.
