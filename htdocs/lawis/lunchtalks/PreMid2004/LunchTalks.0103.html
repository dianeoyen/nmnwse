LUNCHTIME TALK SERIES
 
Tuesday, March 13, 2001
11:30 am - 1:30 pm, Otowi cafeteria side room A, LANL

TALK at 12:00:
Earle Marie Hanson, ESA Division Director, LANL

ABSTRACT

Earle Marie Hanson will discuss her experiences combining parenting with a Lab career. She is currently ESA Division Director, having spent many years as casual and part-time while child-rearing. She's quite articulate about her experiences and supportive of parents who want to maintain Lab careers during child-rearing years.

Nonmembers Welcome!  

Sideroom A is reserved from 11:30 to 1:30 for people who like to eat earlier or later, and informal discussion. You are welcome to come for just the talk at noon, and questions are encouraged!
