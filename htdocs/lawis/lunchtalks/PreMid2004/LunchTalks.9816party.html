ANNUAL MEETING & HOLIDAY PARTY

Wednesday, December 16, 1998
LAWIS Annual Meeting & Holiday PotLuck
6:00PM, 162 Chamisa St, Los Alamos

Wendee Brunish has graciously volunteered to host the LAWIS Annual Meeting and Holiday PotLuck in her home at 162 Chamisa St, Los Alamos.

The schedule will be:

  • 6:00 "cocktails" (pre-meeting social)
  • 6:30 Annual Meeting:
    1) brief review of activities of LAWIS for 1998
    2) announcements of results of the election of new steering committee members
    3) proposal and vote regarding revisions to By-laws/policies
  • 7:00 PotLuck Dinner/Party: bring a dish to serve 8-10 people.

Directions to Wendee's house: On Barranca Mesa, take the first left, and it is the third house on the right: a big gray house with lots of Christmas lights. If you need additional information, contact Wendee at 667-5724, wb@lanl.gov, or 662-3252.

You are welcome to come for just the pre-meeting social, the meeting, or the dinner as your schedule permits. As at all LAWIS events, non-members are also welcome. There is no need to RSVP regarding what you are bringing to the PotLuck: we are counting on the laws of probability to insure you will get a balanced meal: after all, this is a "season of faith" in many religions!
