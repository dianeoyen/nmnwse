FOR THURSDAY APRIL 16 1998

-- LAWIS Field Trip: Los Alamos Women in Science, Northern Chapter of the New Mexico Network for Women in Science and Engineering, will join the SERS/URS students for an all-day field trip to learn about the Geology of the Jemez Mountains (AM) and visit the Milagro Gamma Ray Burst Observatory (PM). The trip will be lead by Carol Hogsett, GRA Representative, LANL. The trip will depart from the Study Center at LANL at 8AM sharp. Advance reservations are strongly recommended as parking is limited at some of the stops. Non-members are welcome. Contact Tinka Gammel to sign up or for more information.
