TALK at 12:00:
Karen Hirsch, NIS-6, LANL
Russian Programs as a Driver for Technology Development

ABSTRACT

The Russian Programs allow interaction between US scientists and Russian scientists. Los Alamos is involved in several different programs with our Russian counterparts. These include programs such as the Highly Enriched Uranium Transparency program that is already working and programs that are still being negotiated and defined. This talk will focus on several of those programs and how those programs have in turn driven technology development in both nuclear detection techniques and data protection techniques.
