WELCOME TO LAWIS

Los Alamos Women in Science is the Northern Chapter of the New Mexico Network for Women in Science. We are a volunteer organzation comprised of women in the STEM fields from all over Northern New Mexico. Our two primary missions are: helping other STEM women through networking events, and organizing and supporting educational outreach programs for Northern New Mexico's youth. In particular, we organize an annual Expanding Your Horizons conference for Northern New Mexico teenage girls. We hope you can join us at our next event! Please browse our site for more information and don't hesistate to contact any of our board members.
