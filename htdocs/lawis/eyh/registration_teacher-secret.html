Adult Sponsors: All students attending EYH16 must have an adult sponsor.
Who is an adult sponsor? -- He/She is a teacher, a counselor, a parent or guardian.
What is the purpose of the adult sponsor? -- The adult sponsor is someone to contact should a question arise as to the eligibility of the
student to attend EYH16, or to recommend the student if the organizers are forced to choose between applicants due to space limitations.
The sponsors do not have to accompany their students to EYH16. However, if the adult sponsor is a Teacher and so chooses, he/she
can register for the same day Teacher Workshop using this form.
