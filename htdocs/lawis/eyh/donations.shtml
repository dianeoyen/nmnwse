Donations:

    Become a sponsor of the Northern New Mexico EYH 2016 conference. We are currently accepting donations to help support this important event. Donations will be used to provide scholarships for the girls, and will cover the cost of providing snacks and breakfast. EYH aims to promote creative and innovative thinkers for the 21st century!

    Donations to the NNM EYH conference are fully tax deductible. The event is sponsored by the Northern and Central Chapters of the New Mexico Network for Women and Science and Engineering (NMNWSE), a non-profit registered 501(c)(3) public charity.
