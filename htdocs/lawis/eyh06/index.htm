EXPANDING YOUR HORIZONS WORKSHOP GOALS:

  • To increase young women's interest in and awareness of mathematics, science, and other nontraditional professions.
  • To provide students an opportunity to meet and form personal contacts with women working in traditionally male occupations.

The Expanding Your Horizons Student Workshop (EYH07) targets 8-10 grade girls. Registrations are accepted on a space available basis. The associated Teacher Conference (TC07) targets teachers accompanying their students to EYH07, but is open to any interested parent or educator.

  2007 information will be available by 1 Jan 2007.   Format will be similar to the 2006 LAWIS EYH.
