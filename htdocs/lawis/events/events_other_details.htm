• Monday, 9 July 2007, 1:00 pm, LANL Seminar:
    Lisa Frehill, Commission on Professionals in Science and Technology (CPST)
    Recruitment, Retention, and Promotion of Women and Minorities
    San Ildefonso Room, LANL Study Center, Los Alamos
          Lisa created the NSF-ADVANCE program at NMSU and through this program has been helping NMT, UNM, NMSU, and LANL increase the number of women and minorities across the job series at each of these institutions. Lisa will be equipped with data and statistics related to Diversity in the workplace and options to help recruit, retain, and promote people in the workplace.
