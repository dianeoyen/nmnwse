June 2003 EYH Fund-Raising Report

by Deborah Potter, Vice President / President-Elect NMNWSE

We received the first grant award for 2004 -- a $5,000 check from the McCune Foundation in Santa Fe. On April 22, 2003, I met with Gary Miller to discuss a potential grant from Sandia Foundation. The meeting was favorable, and he provided some suggestions for future proposals, such as providing statistics for the number of volunteers, students and adults associated with each conference. Although I gave him the EYH testimonial by ³Tammy² posted on our website, he requested additional testimonials. Therefore, letters to Chapters or from archives were requested of Board members.

A thank-you letter was mailed to the McCune Foundation and a grant application was mailed to Albuquerque Community Foundation. We are also waiting to hear whether awards will be granted by [Paul] Newman´s Own, PNM or Sandia Foundation.
