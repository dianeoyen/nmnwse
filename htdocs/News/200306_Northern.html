Northern Chapter Report: June


At the brief "board meeting" before the LAWIS LunchTalk on May 20,
following a brief discussion of the status of the board a vote was taken
of those present which expressed a preference to

(1) have those "current" LAWIS board members as are able continue on the
board until a new board can be elected

(2) allow those "current" board members whose time commitments do not
allow them to continue to gracefully leave the board, with our thanks
for staying on as long as they have

(3) request the NMNWSE board, especially those members based in Los
Alamos, help with re-organizing the LAWIS board and electing a new local
board - possibly smaller and with a refocused scope (focus only on EYH
with the rest of the LAWIS duties relegated to the state board?).
