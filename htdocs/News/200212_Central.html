Albuquerque EYH Report

 

The EYH in Albuquerque will be held on Saturday March 15 at a location to be announced. The keynote speaker is Dr. Joan Woodard, Executive Vice President and the Deputy Director for Sandia National Laboratories. We anticipate about 30 workshops that will accommodate the anticipated 400 girls. There is also an adult program, which features financial aid, how to keep your daughter interested in math and science, and a tour of some UNM labs.
