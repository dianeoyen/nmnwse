Criteria

This prestigious award honors 20 women, selected from statewide nominations, for their exceptional contributions and achievements. Appointed by the governor, judges award women for their extra efforts to improve the status of women in our state. They are rated on community leadership, effectiveness of advocacy for positive change for women and families, and leadership in their careers.

Nomination deadline for 2003 is Friday, February 23

Submission Requirements

Eight complete packets with nomination form, summary and supportive materials.

500 word summary Summarize the nominee's specific achievements, the impact of her contributions to her community and/or the people of New Mexico, the individual style and characteristics she uniquely possesses as a leader, and a brief statement on why she deserves the award. This summary may be used for publication purposes.

Supportive Materials: The material should justify consideration of the nominee. You may want to include biographical data; leadership or community roles with quantifiable results; commitments; mentorship volunteer and compensated advocacy work; and/or newspaper or magazine articles.

A $15 contribution payable to the Commission is welcomed to support the Commission's Conference Fund.
