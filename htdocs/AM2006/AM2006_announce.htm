The 2006 NMNWSE Technical Symposium & Annual Meeting

27-28 OCT 2006

Spirit Canyon Lodge, Lake Roberts, NM
(near Silver City and the Gila Wilderness)

This event will include:

  • Technical Symposium of work and special interests of women working throughout New Mexico
  • Annual Meeting to conduct the business of the Network

2007 NMNWSE Board Voting

Registration
The deadline to register online is Oct 1, 2006.  Papers and online registrations received by COB Monday Oct 2, 2006, will be viewed as meeting the deadline. Post-deadline papers and registrations will be accepted on a space available basis.
After 1 Oct 2006, please submit the online registration form but do not send payment until you receive confirmation from the registrar. Post-deadline papers may be relegated to a poster session - contact the Technical Chair for scheduling of your presentation.
if you are not ready to register, please use the interest survey to let us know if you might come.

Directions
Spirit Canyon Lodge is located at 684 Hwy. 35 at mile marker 22, at the south end of Lake Roberts, NM, about an hour's drive from Silver City. Lake Roberts is a family resort area at the gateway to the Gila Wilderness known for birdwatching and outdoor activites. A map showing the location of the lodge can be found at http://www.spiritcanyon.com/map.html, and the phone number for Spirit Canyon Lodge is (505) 536-9459.
