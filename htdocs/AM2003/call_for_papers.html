2003 Annual Meeting and Technical Symposium: Announcement

October 17-19, 2003
Explora! Science Center and Children's Museum of Albuquerque

The purpose of the symposium is to provide a forum for New Mexico women in technical fields to network by sharing their work and interests through presentations and discussions.

Technical Symposium: Call for Papers

Abstract submission: Please submit an electronic copy of your 200-word abstract to jeannebanks@onevirtualnet.com, or by disk, or hard copy to: NMNWSE Symposium, Jeanne Banks, 14 Leah Lane, Placitas, NM 87043.

Deadline for Submission: September 15, 2003

Notification of Acceptance: September 30, 2003

Presentations: Should not exceed 20 minutes in length with an additional 10 minutes for discussion. If you require audio-visual equipment, please submit this information with your abstract.

Abstracts: Will be reproduced as submitted and distributed at the symposium.
