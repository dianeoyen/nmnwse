  • Northrop Grumman is on the 3rd floor

Heading South on I-25:

  • Take Paseo del Norte exit
  • Go straight to stay on the West frontage road
  • Turn Right on San Francisco, just south of Marriott Pyramid hotel
  • Turn Left on Sun Ave
  • Turn Left at the first corner. Park in the parking lot to your right.
  • Northrop Grumman is on the 3rd floor
Jeanne or someone should be there to direct you to the conference room, or call Jeanne at 505-239-2403 or Tinka at 505-690-1318 if you don't see us.
