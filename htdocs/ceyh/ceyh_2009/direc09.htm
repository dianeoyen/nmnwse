The registration, opening & closing sessions will take place at Dane Smith Hall, on the UNM Central Campus. Access to this area of the campus is through Yale, Las Lomas or Buena Vista. A map of the campus is given at: http://www.unm.edu/campusmap.pdf.

The Hall is labeled with number '48' on this map.

Parking directions will be supplied with the confirmation of registration, sent to each registrant in the US mail. There is FREE parking for the conference.  For buses, please contact a conference coordinator.
