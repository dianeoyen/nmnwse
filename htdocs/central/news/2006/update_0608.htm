Aug 2006 Central Chapter Update

Dear NMNWSE Central Chapter Members and Soon-to-Be Members (I hope):

Please mark your calendars for a wonderful evening of RE-acquainting, RE-newing, RE-creating, and RE-juvenating the Central Chapter of the New Mexico Network for Women in Science and Engineering!! It is Tuesday, August 22 at the St Clair Winery & Bistro on Rio Grande starting at 5:30pm. We won't start the wine tasting until after 6:00 so don't worry if you cannot be there right at 5:30.

  postable flyer for this event

The St Clair Winery & Bistro is located at 901 Rio Grande Blvd. For more information on this establishment, see
http://www.999dine.com/nm/stclair/indexmain.html

Many of you are already Network members and I am sure you will enjoy seeing old friends and meeting new ones. Those of you are not yet members--I know you will want to meet women like yourselves--interested in networking and supporting women in their technical careers. NMNWSE has many initiatives that you will learn about, especially the Expanding Your Horizons conferences.

There will be a great deal of information to share, six wines to taste, wonderful munchies--and a whole lot of fun!! More details about this exciting event will be coming in the next few days. Put it in your Palm right away!! Also--pass it on to your colleagues and friends.

Jeanne Banks, for the Central Chapter
