Nov 2008 Central Chapter Update -- next Central Chapter Dinner Jan 14, 2009

The next central chapter meeting is scheduled for the evening of January 14, 2009 at the Hilton Garden Inn. This will be a special event, as we will be honoring Betsy Frederick, winner of the 2008 NMNWSE IMPACT! Award for her life-long contributions to education. Representatives from the NM Commission on the Status of Women will also be there to help us celebrate Betsy's accomplishments. Exact time, location, and RSVP information will be posted here when available.

Central Chapter members are also encouraged to volunteer to help with the 2009 Central Chapter EYH conference, scheduled for March 14 at UNM main campus. See the EYH webpages for contact information and more details.
