 March Networking Dinner

The March Central Chapter meeting was held on March 15, 2016, at Mimi's Cafe in Albuquerque. Dana Roberson, who has extensive experience in geologic exploration (among her many accomplishments), presented a very interesting talk on "Downtown Albuquerque from a Geologic Point of View" -- a walking tour (in pictures) of the geologic resources viewable in and on buildings in Albuquerque, focusing on the stones used in construction which are primarily from New Mexico and which contribute greatly to the attractiveness of the skyline.
Dana created a map showing the locations discussed in the talk. If anyone wants to see the "geology" for themselves, please contact Jeanne Banks for a copy of the map.
