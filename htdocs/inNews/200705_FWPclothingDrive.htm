Federal Women's Program Annual Clothing Drive

The Federal Women's Program at the Albuquerque DOE site office is sponsoring a clothing drive for women May 7 thru June 8, 2007. I copied the info from their flyer below, and also linked a copy. If you have any questions about the program, please call the listed contacts.
 
If anyone in the Los Alamos or Santa Fe area wants to contribute, I am collecting northern chapter donations - please contact me to arrange pick-up before June 1, as I will be taking the northern chapter contributions down to Albuquerque the weekend of June 2.

ANNUAL CLOTHING DRIVE
May 7 thru June 8, 2007
 
Accepting women's and children's clothing, shoes and accessories.
 
All clothing should be clean and ready for use. Donations may be taken to the NNSA Service Center Warehouse (Bldg 20397) between the hours of 7:30 a.m. and 4:30 p.m. Please place clothing in bags or boxes (no hangers).
 
Women's donations will be taken to the Community Clotheshorse, A New Day Youth & Family Services, and the Albuquerque Rescue Mission -- these agencies provide clothing for needy women. Children's donations will be taken to Peanut Butter and Jelly Family Services and various area schools.
 
For information or assistance contact June Storey, 505-845-6280, or Gloria Maestas-Garcia, 505-845-5979.
 
SPONSORED BY FEDERAL WOMEN'S PROGRAM
