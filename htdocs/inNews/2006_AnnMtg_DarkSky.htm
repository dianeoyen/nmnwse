2006 NMNWSE Annual Meeting Summary
Elections and Dark Sky Resolution

The 2006 NMNWSE Annual meeting was a great success, with about 30 in attendance and 9 technical papers presented. The 2007 board was elected and chairs chosen. The new positions will be posted shortly on the Board webpage. The Dark Sky resolution passed, and an NMNWSE Dark Sky webpage is under construction.
