Nominations for 2012 NMNWSE Board Members Sought

The NMNWSE Board is now accepting nominations for the 2012 Board, which will be elected at the 2011 NMNWSE Annual Meeting to be held the weekend of Nov 4 (note change of date) in Carlsbad, NM. In particular, we are looking for volunteers to serve as President, Vice-President, Secretary, and At-Large Members.

Board meeting are held every other month, and the duties of each position are summarized in our Policies and Procedures.

If you have questions or wish to suggest a candidate (including yourself), please contact the NMNWSE Board, or any board member.
