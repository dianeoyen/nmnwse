2015 NMNWSE IMPACT! Award - Call for Nominations

The NMNWSE IMPACT! Award recognizes NM women for their extraordinary efforts in furthering the goals of the Network: "encouraging women to enter into and development their careers in science, engineering, and allied professions." Nominations are being sought from all geographical areas of our state from individuals, organizations and businesses. Nominators and Nominees do not need to be members of NMNWSE. Nomination forms plus instructions and submission requirements are available at http://nmnwse.org/ImpactAward.
2015 Nominations are due September 14, 2015
