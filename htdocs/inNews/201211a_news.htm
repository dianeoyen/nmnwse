NMNWSE Member MyChelle Andrews Named ABWA National Secretary-Treasurer

The American Business Women's Association elected NMNWSE Member MyChelle Andrews to the position of National Secretary-Treasurer of ABWA during the 63rd annual ABWA National Women's Leadership Conference, held 11-12 Oct 2012 in Memphis, TN.

Andrews, the Data Architect and Business Intelligence Manager for the City of Albuquerque, has a long and distinguished career in Information Technology. With over 25 years' experience, MyChelle has implemented and supported application systems including customer services, human resources and finance. Andrews is currently working on Open Data for an Open Government at the City. By making data more readily available to the citizens of Albuquerque, MyChelle believes she is helping to improve the quality of life in New Mexico.

"I am honored to be elected to the position of National Secretary-Treasurer for ABWA and to represent such a distinguished group of diverse business women. The opportunities available through ABWA have allowed me to excel in my personal life and professional career," says MyChelle. "Working with women leaders across the country has taught me to appreciate diverse management and communication styles. I am able to take this experience and apply the lessons learned with my team at work making our team a more effective and efficient workforce."
