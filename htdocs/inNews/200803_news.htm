April Board Meeting in Socorro at State Science Fair

The April Board Meeting will be held in Socorro on the afternoon of April 5, in Jones Annex room 102 at NMT, following judging of the NMNWSE Awards at the State Science Fair that morning. Interested members and friends are welcome to attend the board meeting - see the Board Meeting webpage for more information.
 
NMNWSE members and friends who would like to help judge the NMNWSE awards should contact the board to arrange meeting us (at about 8am on Apr 5 in the Joseph A. Fidel Student Center at NM Tech), or call Tinka Gammel at 505-690-1318.
