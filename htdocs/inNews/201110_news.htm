2012 NMNWSE Board Elections

Online voting for the 2012 NMNWSE Board is now open. All 2011 members and new 2012 members who pay their dues by Dec 10 2011 may vote. Online voting will close COB Nov 18, 2011. Members may also vote in person at the 2011 Annual Meeting on Dec 10 (see below).

2011 Annual Meeting

The 2011 NMNWSE Annual Meeting has been rescheduled to a combined Annual Meeting and Board Meeting on Saturday, Dec 10, at 10am on the UNM Valencia Campus - see below.

Note this combined meeting will be the first Board Meeting of the 2012 Board, elected as first business of this meeting.
