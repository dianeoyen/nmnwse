LEGO Robotics Competition - Judges, etc needed Nov 8, 2008.

(NM) Lego League volunteers: The First Lego League is looking for judges, referees and volunteers for the New Mexico qualifying competition at UNM's Johnson Gym Saturday, Nov. 8, 9 a.m.-5 p.m. The competition has students aged 9-14 complete projects about climate change and then program robots to complete tasks that relate to that field. Judges and referees will need to attend a short training session before the actual competition. For more information, contact Jessica Coyle (3656) at 245-2137, extension 120, or jlcoyle @ sandia.gov, or see http://nmgirlscouts.org/firstlegoleague.html
