Volunteers to serve on the 2010 NMNWSE Board solicited

Open positions for the 2010 Board (serves Oct 2009 through Oct 2010) include Vice-President/President-Elect and Secretary, plus several At-Large positions. Please consider nominating someone to serve on the executive committee, including yourself. We really need a good slate of Members-at-Large since there were only two elected last year.

Descriptions of board positions and duties can be found in our Policies and Procedures. To nominate someone simply email the NMNWSE Board with your suggestion. Please also feel free to contact the Board if you have any questions (on this or any other issue).
