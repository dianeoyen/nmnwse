Applications sought for NASA WISH program

NASA is looking for young women wanting to become the engineers, scientists, researchers and innovators of tomorrow. The WISH project offers a one-of-a-kind experience for female high school students to jump start their future and explore the possibilities of a Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) related major or career.
