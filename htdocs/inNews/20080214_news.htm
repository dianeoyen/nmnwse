Senft honored as Women On The Move finalist

Congratulations to Donna Cowell Senft of the Air Force Research Laboratory for being selected as a finalist for the 2008 YWCA Middle Rio Grande Women On The Move award!
The winners will be announced on 14 Mar 2008 at the luncheon to honor this year's 22 award finalists, to be held at the Embassy Suites Hotel in Albuquerque.
See YWCA's Women On The Move information page for details.

Governor's Award Nomination deadline
