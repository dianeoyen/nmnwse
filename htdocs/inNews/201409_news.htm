NMNWSE Annual Meeting and Technical Symposium (AM 2014)

The 2014 NMNWSE Annual Meeting and Technical Symposium (AM 2014) will be held October 17-19, 2014 at the historic Plaza Hotel in Las Vegas, NM.
Please visit the AM 2014 webpage for the call for technical presentations and further meeting details. Meeting information will also be included with the annual hardcopy mailing to NMNWSE members, anticipated to go out by Sep. 1.
Registration form for meeting or dinner - Registration is now closed.
If you are not ready to register but might attend, please use the online interest survey if you would like to let us know you might attend, or provide input for meeting planning.
If you plan to attend please consider reserving rooms now. You can call the Plaza Hotel at 505-425-3591 to make reservations for the weekend, mentioning NMNWSE's name to get our prices.
The 2014-15 NMNWSE Board will be elected at AM 2014. If you would be interested in running for the Board, or helping with Board activities, please contact the Elections Chair Yolanda King or any Board member.
 

Call for 2014 IMPACT! Award nominations

 Nomination forms and instructions are posted at nmnwse.org/ImpactAward.
Nominations for the 2014 award must be received by Sep. 15, 2014.
Nominations after that date will be considered for the 2015 award.
The award will be presented at our Annual Meeting to a New Mexico woman for her extraordinary efforts in furthering the goals of the Network: to encourage women to enter into and develop their careers in science, engineering, and allied professions. NMNWSE membership not required. A press release summarizing Jan's contributions will be posted shortly on the IMPACT! Award webpage.
 
UPDATED:  Jan Frigo has been selected as the 2014 IMPACT! Award winner.
