Kim Linder receives 2004 Governor's Award for Outstanding NM Women

Dr. Kim Dalton Linder earned her Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering and Masters and Bachelors Degrees in Electrical Engineering.  She has reached the highest technical level at Honeywell, Senior Staff Engineer.

"Kim demonstrates leadership every day, is approachable, diligent, ethical and caring.  She has volunteered thousands of hours of her time in a wide spectrum of activities to better the lives of people in her community."  Congratulations, Kim!!
