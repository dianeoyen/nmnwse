Nominations sought for 2010 IMPACT! Award

NMNWSE is seeking nominations for our annual IMPACT! Award, given in cooperation with the the New Mexico Commission on the Status of Women to recognize the contributions of New Mexico Women whose actions further the goals of NMNWSE to encourage women to enter into and develop their careers in science, engineering, and allied professions. Nominees do not need to be members of NMNWSE. Nominations forms and more information, including brief biographies of past winners, can be found at http://nmnwse.org/ImpactAward/. The 2010 nomination deadline is Friday, September 17, 2010. The 2010 award will be presented on Oct 9, 2010 at our Annual Meeting and Technical Symposium.

9/15/2010: If you are not able to mail or drop off the nomination packet in time to reach the NM Commission on the Status of Women by COB Friday, Sep 17, we are accepting FAX-ed nominations that arrive by this deadline. See the IMPACT! Award webpage for FAX submission instructions.
