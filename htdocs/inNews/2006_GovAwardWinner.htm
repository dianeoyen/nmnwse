Adrienne Dare receives
2006 Governor's Award for Outstanding NM Women

 Adrienne's nomination letter emphasizing her contributions to women in our state.

  Also nominated in 2006 were NMNWSE members Eleanor Walther and Dana Roberson. Previous Governor Award winners include NMNWSE members Jeanne Banks (2005), Kim Linder (2004), Diane Albert (2003, 2000, 1996), Janie Enter (2003), and Debby Potter (2003).

Congratulations to all!
