Call for Presentations

Abstracts for presentations in the Technical Session the afternoon of 28 Oct 2006 are being considered. Presentations will be 30 minutes, including questions. Contact the Annual Meeting Chair, Dr. Yolanda Jones King, 505-846-4848, or email your abstract to the NMNWSE Board, NMNWSE_BOARD-L@list.unm.edu.

Jeanne Banks and Betsy Frederick receive
2005 Governor's Award for Outstanding NM Women

Jeanne's nomination letter emphasizing her contributions to women in our state and her efforts in the American Business Women's Association (ABWA).
Press release about Jeanne's election as national President of ABWA.
Betsy's nomination letter, emphasizing her efforts to improve educational opportunities for girls, and her pivotal role in starting the NMNWSE EYH conferences, which have reached over 15,000 students and their teachers since 1980.
Betsy's biography

Also nominated in 2005 were NMNWSE members Dana Roberson and Tinka Gammel. Previous Governor Award winners include NMNWSE members Kim Linder (2004), Diane Albert (2003, 2000), Janie Enter (2003), and Debby Potter (2003).

Congratulations to all!

NMNWSE Award Winners in 2004-2005
NM Adventures in SuperComputing Challenge

Local Power Plant Follies by the Shiprock High School Team of Kymberly Brown and Martha Hughes was selected to receive the NMNWSE special award for best project by a woman or team with a majority of women in the 2004-2005 New Mexico Adventures in SuperComputing Challenge. The team was mentored by teachers Vernetta Nobel, Brenda Hines, and Kyla Johnson. NMNWSE is proud to recognize this outstanding project.

NMNWSE also wishes to congratulate all Challenge participants on their many interesting projects!
