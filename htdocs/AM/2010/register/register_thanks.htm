Thank you for registering online
for the 2010 NMNWSE Annual Meeting.

 
  To complete the registration process,  please send a hardcopy of the registration form with your check, payable to NMNWSE, to:

NMNWSE / AM2010
PO Box 1360
Los Alamos, New Mexico, 87544 USA.
If your payment is postmarked Sep 11 or later (Sep 18 or later if applying for a scholarship), the $10 late registration penalty will apply.  After Sep 28, please pay by cash or check at the meeting rather than sending the payment by mail.  (Note we do not accept credit cards.)  If you plan to pay at the meeting rather than mailing your payment, please indicate this in the comment box on the registration form.  (Note the $10 late registration penalty applies for payment at the meeting.)  If you have any questions about the registration fees, please contact the Annual Meeting Chair.


 If you forgot to print a copy, please use the back button on your browser to return to the form.

If you need to make corrections, feel free to re-submit, but please add a note in the comment box that it is a corrected form.


 You should receive an emailed acknowledgment from the registrar indicating we received your online registration within two business days.

If you do not, there may have been server or other problems preventing our receiving your online registration - the printed copy mailed with your check suffices, but we would appreciate your contacting the meeting registrar to give her a heads up.
